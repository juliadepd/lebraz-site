About the App

Vilão is a financial visualization and analysis app. Through Open Banking, we only access read-only data from your accounts to generate metrics, reports and insights about your spending.

We do not move your money. We do not make transfers, payments or any type of transaction. We only read your banking information to help you understand where your money goes.

The connection is made via Open Banking, regulated by the Central Bank of Brazil. You authorize access directly with your bank, without sharing passwords with us.
