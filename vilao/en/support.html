Frequently Asked Questions

Is Vilão safe?

Yes. We use the Open Banking system regulated by the Central Bank of Brazil. Your data is encrypted and we never have access to your bank passwords.

How does the connection to my bank work?

The connection is made through Open Banking Brazil, where you authorize data sharing directly on your bank's website. We only receive the information you authorize.

Can I disconnect my bank at any time?

Yes. You can revoke access to your banking data at any time, both through Vilão and directly with your financial institution.

Does Vilão make transactions on my account?

No. Vilão only views your financial data. We do not perform any type of transaction, transfer or payment on your behalf.

Is my data sold to third parties?

No. We do NOT sell, rent or share your personal or financial data with third parties for marketing purposes.

How do I delete my account?

You can delete your account through the app settings. Your data will be removed within 30 days of the request.
