Privacy Policy

Vilão - Personal Finance Management

Last updated: December 2025

Vilão ("we", "our" or "application") values user privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, store and protect your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

1.1. Information you provide:

  • Name and email (for account creation)
  • App configuration preferences

1.2. Banking information via Open Banking (Belvo):

  • Bank account data (balance, account type)
  • Transaction history
  • Credit card bills
  • Information from connected financial institutions

1.3. Information collected automatically:

  • App usage data
  • Device information (model, operating system)
  • Error logs for service improvement

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Display your consolidated balances and transactions
  • Automatically categorize your expenses
  • Generate personalized financial insights and reports
  • Track savings goals
  • Manage subscriptions and recurring payments
  • Improve user experience

3. Data Sharing

We share data only with:

  • Belvo (Open Banking partner): To access your banking data with your explicit consent, in accordance with Central Bank of Brazil regulations
  • Infrastructure providers: For data hosting and processing

We do NOT sell, rent or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Data Security

We implement security measures including:

  • Data encryption in transit (TLS/SSL) and at rest
  • Secure authentication
  • Storage on security-certified servers
  • Restricted data access by authorized employees

5. Open Banking and Consent

Connection to your bank accounts is made via Open Banking Brazil, regulated by the Central Bank. You can:

  • Choose which banks to connect
  • Revoke access at any time
  • View which data is being accessed

6. Your Rights (LGPD)

Under the Brazilian General Data Protection Law (Law 13.709/2018), you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct incomplete or inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Revoke consent
  • Request portability of data

To exercise these rights, contact us: contato@lebraz.com.br

7. Data Retention

We keep your data while your account is active. After account deletion, your data will be removed within 30 days, except when there is a legal retention obligation.

8. Minors

Vilão is not intended for minors under 18 years of age. We do not intentionally collect data from minors.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes through the application.

10. Contact

Questions about this policy? Contact us:
