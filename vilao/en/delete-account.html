Account and Data Deletion

You have the right to request the deletion of your account and all personal data associated with it, as provided by the Brazilian General Data Protection Law (LGPD - Law 13.709/2018).

What will be deleted

When you request account deletion, the following data will be removed:

  • Profile information (name, email)
  • Synced transaction history
  • Financial goals and objectives
  • App preferences and settings
  • Financial institution connections
  • All data stored on our servers

Warning

This action is irreversible. After deletion, it will not be possible to recover your data. If you only want to disconnect a specific bank account, you can do so in the app settings without deleting your account.

Deletion timeframe

After confirming your request, your data will be deleted within 30 days. You will receive an email confirming the completion of the process.

Data retained by legal obligation

Some data may be retained for an additional period when there is a legal obligation, such as access logs (Brazilian Internet Civil Framework) or data required for compliance with tax or regulatory obligations.

Other ways to request

You can also request account deletion through:

  • Within the app Settings → My Account → Delete Account
  • Email Send an email to contato@lebraz.com.br with the subject "Account Deletion"
  • Support Contact us through our support form
