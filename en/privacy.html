Privacy Policy

Transparency and respect for your data

Last updated: January 2025

1. Introduction

LeBraz (DURAN & GHAZZAOUI LTDA) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our services, applications, and digital platforms.

By using our services, you agree to the practices described in this policy. We recommend reading this document carefully.

2. Data We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Voluntarily provided information: name, email address, phone number, and messages sent through our contact forms.
  • Usage data: information about how you interact with our services, including pages visited, browsing time, and actions taken.
  • Device information: device type, operating system, browser used, and IP address.
  • Cookies and similar technologies: small files stored on your device to improve your browsing experience.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use the collected information to:

  • Respond to your requests and contact messages;
  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services and applications;
  • Personalize your experience on our platforms;
  • Send relevant communications about our services (when authorized);
  • Analyze usage trends for continuous improvement;
  • Comply with legal and regulatory obligations;
  • Protect our rights and prevent fraudulent activities.

4. Data Sharing

LeBraz does not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • Service providers: companies that assist us in operating our services (hosting, data analysis, customer support), always under confidentiality agreements.
  • Legal obligations: when required by law, court order, or competent government authorities.
  • Rights protection: to protect the rights, property, or safety of LeBraz, our users, or the public.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. This includes:

  • Data encryption in transit and at rest;
  • Restricted access controls to personal information;
  • Regular monitoring of our systems;
  • Training our team in data protection practices.

Although we strive to protect your information, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We continuously work to improve our security practices.

6. Your Rights

In compliance with the Brazilian General Data Protection Law (LGPD) and other applicable legislation, you have the following rights:

  • Access: request information about what personal data we hold about you;
  • Correction: request correction of incomplete, inaccurate, or outdated data;
  • Deletion: request the elimination of your personal data, when applicable;
  • Portability: request the transfer of your data to another provider;
  • Consent revocation: withdraw your consent for data processing at any time;
  • Opposition: object to the processing of your data in certain circumstances.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us through the channels indicated below.

7. Cookies

We use cookies and similar technologies to improve your browsing experience, analyze site traffic, and personalize content. The types of cookies we may use include:

  • Essential cookies: necessary for basic site functionality;
  • Performance cookies: collect information about how visitors use the site;
  • Functionality cookies: allow the site to remember your choices and preferences.

You can configure your browser to refuse cookies, although this may affect some site functionality.

8. Data Retention

We keep your personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, including legal, contractual, and regulatory obligations. When your data is no longer needed, it will be securely deleted or anonymized.

9. Privacy Contact

If you have questions, concerns, or requests related to this Privacy Policy or the processing of your personal data, please contact us:

  • Email: privacidade@lebraz.com.br
  • General email: contato@lebraz.com.br
  • Address: Av. Paulista, 1000 - Suite 501, Bela Vista, São Paulo - SP, ZIP 01310-100, Brazil

10. Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We recommend that you review this page regularly to stay informed about how we protect your information.

Significant changes will be communicated through our website or by other appropriate means. The date of the last update will always be indicated at the beginning of this document.
